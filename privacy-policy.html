At HomeHero, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Names, avatars, and family member roles
  • Contact Information: Email addresses for account recovery and support
  • Apple ID Information: When using Sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID and associated email

Usage Data

  • Task Completion Data: Tasks completed, XP earned, completion dates and times
  • Family Activity: Duels, rewards redeemed, streak information
  • App Usage: Features used, session duration, app performance data
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version

Premium Features Data

  • Photo Evidence: Images uploaded for task verification (Premium feature)
  • Analytics Data: Detailed usage patterns and productivity insights (Premium feature)
  • Subscription Information: Billing history, subscription status, payment methods

2. How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Provide and maintain the HomeHero service
  • Track task completions and family progress
  • Calculate XP, levels, and achievements
  • Enable family duels and competitions
  • Manage rewards and redemptions

Premium Features

  • Provide advanced analytics and insights
  • Enable photo verification for tasks
  • Generate detailed progress reports
  • Offer enhanced family management tools

Communication and Support

  • Send important app updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Respond to your inquiries and feedback

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

HomeHero primarily stores your family's data locally on your device using SwiftData. This means:

  • Your family's task and activity data stays on your device
  • We don't have access to your personal family information
  • Data is not transmitted to external servers unless explicitly needed

Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you enable iCloud backup, your HomeHero data may be included in your device's iCloud backup, subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Security Measures

  • Data encryption both in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication through Apple's Sign in with Apple
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No third-party data sharing without explicit consent

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Limited Sharing

We may share information only in these specific circumstances:

  • App Store Services: Subscription management through Apple's App Store
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist in app operation (under strict confidentiality)

5. Children's Privacy

HomeHero is designed for families with children. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • Parental consent is required for children under 13
  • Parents control all family data and can delete children's information
  • We collect minimal information necessary for app functionality
  • No advertising or third-party tracking of children

6. Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all your family's data within the app
  • Edit Information: Update family member information anytime
  • Delete Data: Remove family members or delete your entire account
  • Export Data: Premium users can export family analytics and reports

Account Deletion

You can delete your HomeHero account at any time through the app settings. This will:

  • Permanently delete all family data from your device
  • Cancel any active subscriptions
  • Remove your account from our systems
  • Cannot be undone

7. Subscription and Billing Privacy

For Premium subscriptions:

  • Billing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
  • We receive only subscription status information
  • Your payment information is never shared with us
  • Subscription management occurs through your Apple ID settings

8. International Users

HomeHero is available worldwide. If you're using our app outside the United States:

  • Your data remains on your local device
  • We comply with applicable international privacy laws
  • GDPR rights apply to European users
  • Contact us for specific regional privacy questions

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll notify you through the app
  • The updated policy will be posted on our website
  • Your continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
  • Significant changes will require explicit consent
